2023 Outdoor tournament

This year, Botany Hammer participated in the 2023 AKSS Outdoor Ultimate Championships. There were around 17 teams from all over Auckland who took part outdoors at Bruce Pulman Park to find this years Outdoor Ultimate Frisbee championships. This was the first tournament for many of our new players and they all adapted very well to playing against our opponents. Luckily, there was optimal weather conditions, as the wind and sun were not too strong so our team was able to play to our best abilities. Despite, not placing our team tried their very best and had loads of fun during the tournament and had many beneficial learning experiences along the way.

2023 Indoor tournament

Botany Hammer participated in the 2023 AKSS Indoor Ultimate Championships. There were 17 teams who took part in the event. In this tournament, Botany Hammer was divided into BDSC Maroon and BDSC Navy. Both our teams played to the best of their ability, and we come out with wonderful results! Since Ultimate Frisbee is a self-refereed sport, each tournament awards the team with the best sportsmanship, respect for their opponents and attitude, the Spirit of the Game medals. Our team, BDSC Maroon won the Spirit of the Game awards which was an amazing accomplishment!

Kohi Winter Indoor League

This year, a small group of players from the BDSC Ultimate Frisbee team are participating in the Kohi Winter Indoor League held at the Barfoot and Thompson stadium. This league runs for approximately 10 weeks with a focus on beginner and intermediate levels who are looking for a socially competitive experience. Botany Hammer plays against teams of all ages, including adult-only teams. The league is 4 a side with the rule that there must be 2 males and 2 females on the court at all times. This league is a great learning experience for all our players and is a very enjoyable experience for all.
